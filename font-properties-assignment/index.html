what Excatly Is Green Tea?

All tea comes from the same plant, Camellia sinensis, but the leaves are processed differently to make green, black, and oolong tea.

how to make green tea?

  1. Place the green tea bag into the cup.
  2. Heat the water at 80-85 degrees celsius and pour into the cup.
  3. Cover the cup for 3 minutes.
  4. Remove the lid and the tea bag, and stir the tea.
  5. Have a sip. The tea is ready!

benefits of drinking green tea?

  • Increased Mental Alertness
  • Protection Against Heart Disease
  • Lower Cholesterol
  • Cancer Prevention
  • Reduced Risk of Diabetes

the possible side effects of drinking too much green tea

Because of the caffeine that contains inside the green tea, drinking too much of it can lead to classic signs of caffeine overconsumption, such as feeling jittery and having trouble with sleep.
